Our Mission

Recreating Revenge Of The Sith In Minecraft.

Or a little bit more detailed: our mission is to bring the excellent work that George Lucas has done into minecraft. Our first target is to recreate Star Wars - Revenge of the Sith and translate it into minecraft. We don't plan to do this as an animation(blender, cinema4d, etc.) but rather in-game. Everything will be recorded in minecraft and then edited - so the space ships are flying, the blaster actually shoots and so on. But before we do that we are creating a trailer as an proof of concept and effect test ... and a star wars trailer in minecraft itself is already pretty cool.
